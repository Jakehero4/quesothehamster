Queso is a playful and adventurous Syrian Albino Hamster. Known for his friendly nature, he enjoys burrowing in his bedding, running on his wheel, and most of all eating food.

Queso has been on the Justin Willman 2024 Illuminati Tour. He has been a vital figure of the Portland showing.
