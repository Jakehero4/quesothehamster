Heidi

Breed: Syrian Albino Hamster

Color: White

Biography

Heidi is a playful and curious Syrian Albino Hamster. Known for her friendly nature, she enjoys burrowing in her bedding, running on her wheel, and nibbling on sunflower seeds.

Her favorite pastime is exploring new tunnels and taking afternoon naps. Despite her small size, Heidi has a big personality and always brings joy to those around her.
